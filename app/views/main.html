Why Craftsman's Passion?

We’re a group of professional software craftsmen that gets things done, no fluff. We don't have managers, analysts, testers nor simply coders. Our people are capable of building hotsy-notsy products from scratch. We’re open and transparent to you — when we see something that sucks, we’re telling you immediately. We value simplicity and spend time on making things simple. We do not cut corners even if someone wants us to — we stand behind our work that is of top-notch quality. In fact, we’re not for everyone. We’re for those who seek to be step ahead.

Skilled people, sterling team play and a disciplined approach to software delivery are the keys to building great systems. Join one of our open trainings or let us organise exclusive event for your process.

We build software. Make your idea real.

Yes, we share the same feeling as you do — it's pity to see how nowaday's software development looks like. Remember weeks spent on writing specifications, unfulfilled expectations, delays, poor quality, huge development and maintenance costs, arguing with stubborn developers that don't get your ideas right. Step back, we can't fix mainstream because it's fundamentally broken. "Agile" doesnt't help much, as it's applied on top of the broken model. Such phenomenon reminds us putting lipstick on a pig. That's why we offer fresh and opinionated view on software development, leaving mainstream behind. We eliminate artificial walls between business & product-oriented IT experts which leads to close and creative collaboration. In this way, your product gains the best from both worlds. Add transparency, excellent quality, short feedback cycles and some fun to the soup and you'll have the best software development journey ever.

We bootstrap projects. Drive the right way.

Chosen architecture, technological stack, methodologies and team determines sustainable evolution of your project. We prepare stable foundation you can continue building on top of — be it architecture, best practices or testing process. Besides bootstrapping new stuff, we’re ready to come for rescue when your existing software got derailed. We put it back on rails and eliminate the root cause of incident. Enjoy your trip!

We protect projects. Drive safe.

Software development usually reminds the boiling frog story. We work side by side with you, watch your back and protect your project from getting boiled. It's our duty to reveal and prevent problems as early as possible and make sure your project is done in a timely, professional manner. It's like having an insurance. But unlike insurance that mitigate failures, we prevent them from happening.

We advise. Achieve your goal.

Over the years of being part of great projects, as well as weak ones, we learned what makes the difference. We provide consultancy services to find the most effective way for achieving your goals. Together we can recognise what is the right thing to do and how to get it right.
