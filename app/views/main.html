Practical Domain-Driven Design

A domain model lies in the heart of every enterprise application. Nowadays developers spend too much time on "surrondings", tackling deeply technical, challenging issues. However, it's a core domain that makes business spin, therefore deserves extra attention. Domain-Driven Design or DDD is an excellent way to tackle complexity in the heart of software and make it far more understandable and manageableby by connecting software model to the mental model of end-users. Unfortunately, developers find DDD pretty hard to grasp because of poorly-written materials available online, lack of good examples and industry-spread experience. Because of this, vitally important practice is left aboard, making the system rigid, fragmented and hard-to-adapt. In fact, DDD is pretty simple if attacked from the right perspective. Learn how to do both tactical and strategical DDD right.

How it's gonna look like?

We'll explore DDD techniques based on real-world examples and a working Java project, discuss and exchange experience. You will be sent the working Java project for further independent analysis.

How long it's gonna take?

2 days.

Am I eligible?

Yes if you're seasoned developer / architect / team leader willing to understand DDD thoroughly. Knowledge of Java programming language is desirable.

Specification By Example

Specification by example is a collaborative approach to defining requirements as executable examples instead of abstract statements. It is applied in the context of agile software development methods. This approach is particularly successful for managing requirements and functional tests on large-scale projects of significant domain and organisational complexity. The workshop is based on Gojko Adzic's books "Bridging the Communication Gap" and "Specification by Example", as well as our personal experience developing, leading and testing software development projects using this approach.

How it's gonna look like?

We provide interactive workshop aimed to tell you about tools and practices that can help adopting Specification by example practice in your daily job and eliminate communication gap between all parties involved in software development process.

How long it's gonna take?

2 days.

Am I eligible?

Yes if you're developer / QA engineer / team leader / business analyst willing to improve comminucation inside your software development project and organisation.

Software Architecture for Developers

In the age of agile software development and cross-functional teams, everyone is an architect. Software architecture is not about big up-front design with UMLs and circles on the whiteboard - it's about practices and techniques you have to follow in order to keep design clean under a rain of changing requirements, things that are extremely important for fighting design entropy. As a professional developer you should know how to cope with evolutionary design and avoid "code & fix" insanity. Spread your knowlege beyond coding — learn how to lead the way and do "just enough" software architecture by youself. Define, ensure and articulate software architeture in your team, maintain technical vision and keep balance with agility.
