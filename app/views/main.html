Software Architecture Essentials

In the age of agile software development and cross-functional teams, everyone is an architect. Software architecture is not about big up-front design with UMLs and circles on the whiteboard - it's about practices and techniques you have to follow in order to keep design clean under a rain of changing requirements, things that are extremely important for fighting design entropy. As a professional developer you should know how to cope with evolutionary design and avoid "code & fix" insanity. Spread your knowlege beyond coding — learn how to lead the way and do "just enough" software architecture by youself. Define, ensure and articulate software architeture for applications in your team, maintain technical vision and keep balance with agility.

  • Learn what does it mean to be an architect and why the role is so important
  • Learn how to connect code with architecture
  • Learn why layered architecture usually suck and what to do with it
  • Apply modern approaches to modularity, platform development and extensibility
  • Distill domain knowledge into evolving model using DDD
  • Benefit from modern architectural approaches such as micro-services, CQRS, Event Sourcing, REST and many others
  • Learn essential software architecture patterns
  • Learn how to build resilient and failure-embracing systems
  • Learn how to choose the right software architecture and equilize fancyness with pragmatism
  • Learn how to incorporate software architecture and design efforts to your daily work

... and much more.
